Wireless Access · ICT

Turning a lock into a platform

The Problem

Customers managing large access control deployments needed real-time visibility into door events and device health — but the product delivered data in disconnected silos.

My Approach

Led cross-functional alignment across firmware, cloud, and UX teams to define a unified data model. Drove the roadmap from concept through global NPI — from hardware specs to digital dashboard experience.

The Outcome

Launched connected wireless lock platform across multiple global markets, expanding from standalone device to operational intelligence layer.

Lifecycle Strategy · Global

NPI from hardware to dashboard

The Problem

NPI was failing to close the loop between physical deployment and digital adoption — teams shipped hardware but had no insight into how customers were using it.

My Approach

Implemented SAVE International Value Methodology (MOD 1) to reframe the product around customer outcomes. Built lifecycle tracking into the NPI process itself.

The Outcome

Created a repeatable NPI playbook connecting physical delivery to digital utilization metrics — giving the business post-launch visibility for the first time.
